Contributor Guidelines

Adding your own custom sub-page is fast and easy. Follow these steps:

  1. 1
    Clone the repo: Clone this codebase locally and navigate to the project root.
  2. 2
    Create a folder: Duplicate the template folder contributors/_template/ and rename it to your preferred namespace (e.g. contributors/your-name/).
  3. 3
    Customize content: Edit your index.html, style.css, and script.js files inside your new folder. Add all assets locally.
  4. 4
    Fill out Metadata: Open your folder's metadata.json and set your author name, project title, tags, description, and GitHub user.
  5. 5
    Commit and Push: Commit your directory. On push, GitHub Actions will automatically scan your files, compile the list, and deploy the updated showcase to the web!
To preview your submission locally before pushing, run node build.js in your terminal to compile the index, then open the landing page.
