Chapter 1: DEBATE ME

Chapter Text

NANAKA YUE

Nanaka Yue, the proclaimed heir of the Yue clan, was a lifestyle content creator. A few months before her proclamation as the next in line for the clan she’d start it as a hobby. From all the knowledge she’d accumulated from books and all the topics she loved to question. Slowly she turned into a big sister for the internet.

Boy advice, life advice or just the reason to go on. People listened. Something she never experienced before in her stingy clan.

It was refreshing for her.

Being an influencer had its ups and downs and today was definitely a down. Her phone was overflowing with notifications. Notifications are normal but there were so many. That she had to check even if it was her day off.

The Zenin Clan Heir and the Yue clan heir beef?!!

Naoya Zenin speaks about Nanaka yue on his podcast ‘The Natural Order’

⤷ clip of 50 secs linked

No wonder her phone was blowing up. This fucker. What was his deal? He’d been talking about her for a while now, always hinting about her and her content but had never said her name out loud. She’d always assumed he’d never say it.

If he had a higher iq instead of a high ego then maybe he wouldn’t have been so brass.

The Yue clan and Zenin clan were ‘talking’ after years of absolutely despising each other.

Bringing politics to social media is a stupid call. Even for Naoya, She assumed it was her lack of response that must've fueled him to finally spit out her name on his podcast.

About being unapologetically yourself to the fullest.

This wasn't very her.

His slimy fans came to poke at her under posts and stories. Her fans were questioning her beliefs. This was starting to genuinely frustrate her.

Nanaka and Naoya were both on opposite sides of the content creation spectrum where one would spread hate the other would uplift, motivate. Their clan were on opposite sides as well. The Yue clan cared all about power. If you weren’t powerful, you were deemed unfit and stripped of the Yue name.

The Yue clan had pushed Nanaka to be the ruthless special grade sorcerer she is today but they had their problems, their hatred towards her job for one as an influencer, they’d call her that in complete disgust or her refusal to procreate as most women in clans would, especially with her rare power, Matter Manipulation.

She clearly had no reason to waste her 20s with fucking kids.

It made things ten times worse that the heir of the Zenin clan was also trying to fuck with her on the internet. Did he not have anything better to do??

Nanaka groaned into her hand, annoyance etched across her face as she opened the notification and clicked on Naoya’s stupid podcast.

Naoya lounged back against the sofa, dressed in a dark navy polo layered over a red plaid shirt, the collar slightly undone like he couldn’t be bothered to fix it. A thin black necklace rested against his neck matching his earrings, catching just enough attention without trying.

Honestly Nanaka assumed He’d wear traditionals on his alpha podcast as well but unfortunately for her he had fashion and it disgusted her to dwell on it any further.

Naoya sighed, grumbling to his chat as he pulled his mic closer to his mouth, “Female influencers continuously are brain washing woman, They clearly refuse to acknowledge their place and go out of the way to tell our young woman to be more” He paused, stretching the next word with a mocking tone “independent”

He said the word independent looking into the camera as if he was trying to say something from his actions instead of his mouth.

“Most of them don’t have a boyfriend because they are too dependent like that bitch–Nanaka Yue,half her videos are some bullshit about self-worth and confidence.” He laughed as if it was the funniest thing in the world. “I can’t believe women are this stupid”

She wasn’t surprised he’d bring up something so trivial to her as a point. It was common knowledge that girls from clans married early but Nanaka had no reason to. She did wonder though, How’d he know something like that? No one out of the clan would willinging talk down about Nanaka, at least not openly.

This let out a guttural groan out of her mouth, She was completely frustrated.

The clip ends with Naoya reaction to chats comments flowing in “call that bitch out” “Nanaka is bad but only if her mouth is shut” “WW IN THE CHAT”

He was degrading her whole brand and she couldn't even respond.

Nanaka laughed at the stupidity of it. Of him, the situation.

He knew why she never said anything. Their clans were in discussions for an arranged marriage, it was an alliance of some sort more than a marriage. A huge deal in their world so much so that the Gojo clan acted as a mediator between their conflicting beliefs.

Keeping quiet would be better for both of them, But clearly Naoya didn’t care about it

Nanaka thought about typing back a snark comment, something to fuel hatred towards his side just like he did to her. A temporary solution but just before she could think of anything a call comes from Gojo Satoru.

“Are you calling about Zenin?” she asked, her voice edged with irritation. “I’m really not in the mood”

A quiet laugh crackled through the phone. “Yeah” Satoru said “I figured.”

The Blue-eyed ‘freak’ as she liked to call him was one of her closer friends from the Gojo Clan, Gojo Satoru. He was playful but cunningly so

Nanaka, dead calm, leaned back in her chair. “Then say what you want. However, I'm not going to just stay quiet anymore.”

“Maybe I wanted to check up on a close friend” He replied amused by her bitterness

She scoffed. Her blood seemed to boil now.

“Why should I play by the rules and he doesn’t? If he can’t shut up then why the fuck should I?”

“I don’t expect any rationalism from him. But you know me, I'm not going to take it, Gojo, I’m just not.”

A long sigh left his mouth. She could feel the resignation from him and found it in herself to sympathise with her friend. Being a mediator for these clans is no joke; And from what she heard it’s going nowhere.

“You’re still pretending this alliance is going somewhere?” She squeaked.

“It’s worth the effort” Gojo huffed a quiet laugh, but it didn’t quite reach his eyes.

“It’s that bad?”

“When is it ever good?”

“Let me guess,” she muttered, her eyes rolling. “Zenin won’t compromise.”

“Neither will Yue.” He retorted, letting out a sigh of pure annoyance.

“Of course we wouldn’t” Nanaka replied harsher getting riled up “We don’t send our strongest for marriages, They have better thing to do like fight and–”

“Yeah well this is politics for you.” Satoru interrupted

“More like babysitting”

A silence stretched between them. She sighed, the fight in her eyes dimming just a little. She could hurt Naoya in other ways than just social media, It wouldn’t be the first time she'd hurt him physically.

"Fine. I’ll stay out of it. Keep things quiet at least from his side just for a while.”

“Nah.”

She blinked. “What?”

“Go start online drama”

Nanaka straightened slightly. Confused by her friend's change of heart

“Satoru.”

“I’m serious,” he said, voice lighter now but there was something deliberate underneath.

“He name-dropped you because he mostly doesn't think the alliance will happen either, Besides He isn’t that stupid. And I know how hard it is for you to keep quiet but with the way things are going I doubt this drama would even matter.”

A skeptical look flashed through her face. A bit bewildered by Gojo's acceptance “You’re not helping your case as a mediator.”

“I think I gave up on that,” he replied lazily. “Besides the clans have much bigger issues”

Nanaka let out a short laugh. “You’re insane.”

“Maybe,” he said. “But knowing you, you already have a plan”

She didn’t answer.

Nanaka replied to Satoru with a low guttural laugh , the sound filling her room. “Maybe I do”

Her anger had been simmering for far too long, building up to this moment. Something deep within her relished the idea of ruining Naoya. Just like she did before.

If he wanted a reaction, she’d give him one just not the way he expected. Slowly, deliberately, she’d strip his ego away, piece by piece, until there was nothing left for him to hide behind.

After some more banter between the two the call ends an evil smile filling Nanaka’s lips as she came up with what she wanted to say, she tweets

From @Nanakayue

Debate me you sad pathetic excuse of a man @naoyazenin

⠂uploaded

Nanaka decided to put her phone down after that. She knew Naoya’s team was probably in her DMs or maybe he’d reply to her tweet. Either way, he’d gotten the reaction he wanted. But she wasn’t about to give anything more just yet , She wanted him to wait. The thought of Naoya waiting for her reply soothes in her chest, she’s always loved playing hard games.
